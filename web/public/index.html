Waiting for tournament to begin

Once the 2026 NCAA Tournament tips off, real game results will be recorded here automatically. Each mode's pre-tournament predictions will be scored against actual outcomes using Brier score and log loss.

How it works:

  • Before each round, MadnessEngine records every mode's win probabilities
  • After games are played, actual results are compared against those predictions
  • Modes are ranked by prediction accuracy across all rounds
  • Check the bracket view for per-game accuracy indicators once results are in
